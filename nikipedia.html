Nick Turani is an American podcaster and content creator best known for his work with Barstool Sports. He co-hosts A New Untold Story with KB NoSwag and is a regular contributor to The Yak. His style blends dry humor, surreal sketches, and deadpan delivery.

Early Life

Turani was born in Wheeling, West Virginia. He ran cross country in high school, recording a personal best of 25:41.5 in the 5K at the Wild and Wet Invitational in 2006[citation needed]. He attended the same school as KB NoSwag, though their friendship developed later.

Career

Turani joined Barstool Sports in 2019. Initially hired for graphic design, he transitioned to on-camera roles after submitting a portfolio of Photoshop memes[citation needed]. He has since appeared in multiple Barstool shows and podcasts.

  • The Yak: A daily live show featuring improvisational comedy and recurring bits.
  • A New Untold Story: A podcast known for its unconventional format and surreal humor.
  • The Bracket: A debate-style show ranking topics such as “Best Empire” and “Worst Smell.”
  • Rough N’ Rowdy: Turani serves as a ringside reporter, often in costume.

Controversies

  • Wake Up Mintzy Appearance: Turani’s guest spot on the revived Wake Up Mintzy drew attention due to the show’s prior cancellation and chaotic tone.
  • Bracket Rankings: His inclusion of “gluten allergies” and “the HBO logo” in a “Most Hated Things” bracket sparked debate among fans[citation needed].
  • Wheeling Origin Dispute: Some fans question whether Turani and KB actually grew up together or if it’s a long-running bit[citation needed].

Style and Persona

Turani is known for his dry delivery and commitment to character. He frequently uses invented metrics such as “Confusion Index” and “Laugh Velocity” to describe content[citation needed].

Trivia

  • Turani once attempted to name 100,000 things in a live stream.
  • He has never publicly confirmed a nickname.
  • His Photoshop hobby includes creating fake cereal boxes and alternate movie posters.

Legacy

Turani’s work at Barstool Sports is characterized by its unpredictability and commitment to absurdity. His influence is felt across multiple shows and formats, particularly in the blending of satire and sincerity.
